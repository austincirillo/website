Where are you from?

I was born in Lansing Michigan, but have lived in Syracuse, New York, ever since I was only a few months old. I grew up becoming a huge fan of the Syracuse Orangemen, our city's basketball team. Other than sports, Syracuse is known for its crazy weather. We hit nationwide high snowfall levels every year. In fact, I just checked and we are number 1.

Why Washington?

Internships, internships, internships. I was not 100% committed to a career path coming out of high school. I had interests in politics, history, writing, and so forth, but I had no specific goal. After applying to many schools, I decided to attend American University because of its academic standing and perfect location in Washington, D.C. It turns out this was a great decision, because I have taken the opportunity to do several internships and have taken many courses that have shifted my interests.

Future goals?

I am just about completed with my junior year of college. In the near future, I plan on attending graduate school if the cost is not too high and if the univeristy I would attend seems worthwhile. Otherwise, I plan on joining the workforce after narrowing down which internship I liked the best, and which tools from American University I enjoyed learning the most. In the long term after I am able to secure a job, I plan on moving somewhere warmer. Remember, I'm from Syracuse.
